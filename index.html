Our Story

Prabha Tea began as a small family venture in the foothills of the Himalayas. What started as a passion for sharing fresh, high-quality teas with friends and neighbours soon grew into a commitment to bring the same experience to tea lovers across the world.

We work closely with partner gardens and smallholder farmers who have been nurturing tea bushes for generations. Our teas are vacuum packed within hours of harvest, preserving the natural oils and aromas that make Indian tea truly special.
